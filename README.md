# Bounty Run

A small browser game: you're a getaway driver in a top-down city.
Drive over red **crime spots** to rack up bounty — the more you steal,
the higher your **wanted level**, and the more police cars will chase you.
Get caught and the run ends.

## Play

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. No build step, no dependencies.

## Controls

- **WASD** or **Arrow keys** — drive
- **Space** — handbrake
- Lay low (no crimes for a while) to cool your heat down

## Goal

Beat your high score. It's saved locally in your browser.
